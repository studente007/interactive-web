My Story

a story of Mine

So now that you know why most people on the planet are addicted to coffee. It is time for my story. My first time trying coffee was when my uncle gave me some at his house.

First sip was so bad i got traumatized by it immediatetly.That was when i was a 7 years old kid. I grew up in the carribean which people normally don't drink that much coffe in the first place and secondly my parent are chinese, so i didn't got the chance try coffee again.

Everything change when i came to The Netherlands. The Netherlands is one of the top coffee drinking countries in the world as explained by the video of CGP Grey. I started drinking coffee because people around are, but also because i need the cafeine to keep me awake for my study here. Then there is where everything started.
